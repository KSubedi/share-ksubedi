Jensen Huang on Open Source AI **Date:** July 24, 2026 **Platform:** X (formerly Twitter) #

Key Statement
"
For my first post, I'm sharing a letter @NVIDIA signed on why open models matter. AI will transform every industry, power every company, and be built by every country. Open models strengthen safety and cybersecurity, accelerate innovation and diffusion, and enable sovereignty. The world needs both frontier closed models and frontier open models." #

Coalition The letter titled **"Open Weights and American AI Leadership"** was co-signed by 25 technology organizations including:
  • Microsoft
  • Meta
  • IBM
  • Palantir
  • Hugging Face
  • Linux Foundation #

    Policy Position Jensen Huang and the coalition urged U.S. lawmakers to:
  • Avoid premature restrictions or regulatory clamp-downs on downloadable open-weight AI models
  • Recognize that over-regulation would stifle competition and push innovation abroad
  • Support both frontier closed models and frontier open models as complementary approaches #

    Notable Absences OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google did not sign the letter.
