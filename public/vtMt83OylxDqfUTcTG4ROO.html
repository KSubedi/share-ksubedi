Build Your First AI Voice Agent

45-minute hands-on workshop
Workshop Handout · Complete 24 hours before class

About This Workshop

AI voice agents are transforming how businesses handle inbound calls, but the landscape is fragmented: dozens of platforms, competing architectures, and no clear starting point. This workshop cuts through the noise.

You'll learn the universal principles behind every voice agent platform — instruction design, knowledge grounding, call routing, tool integration, analytics, and compliance — then apply them immediately by building a working agent.

The hands-on portion uses echowin (no-code, plain-English training, free to start), but every concept, framework, and best practice transfers directly to Vapi, Retell, Synthflow, Bland, or any other platform you choose. Walk out with a live agent you can call, plus a mental model that works everywhere.

What You'll Learn

🧱 Understand the universal voice agent architecture: instructions, knowledgebase, tools, routing, and channels
✍️ Design effective agent instructions using the Greeting → Disambiguation → Resolution → Close framework
🔨 Build a working voice agent from scratch and deploy it to a phone number
🔀 Configure call routing, screening, and human handoff patterns that apply across platforms
📊 Read voice agent analytics: which metrics matter, what targets to aim for, and how to close the improvement loop
💼 Evaluate white-label and agency business models for any voice platform
🛡️ Recognize voice agent limitations and apply platform-agnostic workarounds

Who Should Attend

Technical founders, agency owners, solutions engineers, and product builders. No prior voice AI experience required. Bring a laptop and a phone.

Build It: Hands-On

Everyone builds a working agent. Step 1: Write instructions using the Greeting → Disambiguation → Resolution → Close pattern (works on every platform). Step 2: Build a knowledgebase — website sync, document upload, FAQ pairs (every platform has an equivalent). Step 3: Connect tools — scheduling, SMS, email, webhooks (universal integration patterns). Step 4: Deploy to a phone number. Step 5: Place a live test call. Target: first working call within 15 minutes.

Build on echowin; patterns transfer everywhere.

Call Routing, Screening & Handoff

Generative routing vs. keyword routing — the tradeoffs every platform faces. Skills-based routing: "billing → accounting," "appointments → scheduler." Call screening patterns: block spam, prioritize VIPs. Human handoff: when to transfer, how to preserve context, the warm-transfer pattern.

Concepts universal; demo on echowin.

Analytics

The metrics every voice platform should give you: containment rate, CSAT, escalation rate, average handle time, hallucination rate. Live monitoring: active calls, sentiment, transcription. The improvement loop: review recordings → identify failure patterns → update instructions → retest. How echowin's Business Compass turns conversations into trends, anomalies, and revenue signals automatically — plus weekly AI-generated digests so you don't have to review every call.

Concepts universal; demo on echowin.

The Agency Business Model

How to package and sell voice agents regardless of platform. Pricing models: per-minute, per-call, bundled tiers, annual prepaid. Margins: charge 2–5× infrastructure cost; target 60–80% gross margin. White-label economics: sub-accounts, client isolation, custom branding. The recurring-revenue play: monitoring + maintenance retainers.

Concepts universal; pricing examples from multiple platforms.
