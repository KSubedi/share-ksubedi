About This Workshop

AI voice agents are transforming how businesses handle inbound calls, but the landscape is fragmented: dozens of platforms, competing architectures, and no clear starting point. This workshop cuts through the noise.

You'll learn the universal principles behind every voice agent platform - instruction design, knowledge grounding, call routing, tool integration, analytics, and compliance - then apply them immediately by building a working agent.

The hands-on portion uses echowin (no-code, plain-English training, free to start), but every concept, framework, and best practice transfers directly to Vapi, Retell, Synthflow, Bland, or any other platform you choose. Walk out with a live agent you can call, plus a mental model that works everywhere.

What You'll Learn

🧱 Understand the universal voice agent architecture: instructions, knowledgebase, tools, routing, and channels
✍️ Design effective agent instructions using the Greeting → Disambiguation → Resolution → Close framework
🔨 Build a working voice agent from scratch and deploy it to a phone number
🔀 Configure call routing, screening, and human handoff patterns that apply across platforms
📊 Read voice agent analytics: which metrics matter, what targets to aim for, and how to close the improvement loop
💼 Evaluate white-label and agency business models for any voice platform
🛡️ Recognize voice agent limitations and apply platform-agnostic workarounds

Course Outline

🎙️
0:00 – 3:00

Why Voice Agents

  • The inbound call problem every business faces: missed calls, after-hours gaps, repetitive FAQs burning staff hours
  • Why voice agents are the answer - and why now (latency is finally low enough, LLMs are finally good enough)
  • The four-layer architecture every platform shares: Instructions → Knowledge → Tools → Channels
  • Why the greeting is everything: the first 5 seconds set caller expectations, establish trust, and determine whether they stay on the line - it's the single most important design decision you'll make. A weak greeting loses callers before the agent ever gets to help them
  • How echowin embodies this with plain-English training, auto-research from your website, and one-click deployment across phone, chat, WhatsApp, and SMS
  • Live demo: call a pre-built echowin agent so everyone hears the end result before building
🎯
3:00 – 8:00

Where Voice Agents Win

  • The framework that works on any platform: "what happens to the calls you miss today?"
  • Real use cases: 24/7 reception, appointment scheduling, lead qualification, after-hours coverage, FAQ deflection
  • Atypical use cases: internal IT helpdesk, patient intake triage, real estate showing scheduler, order status by phone, multilingual customer support, restaurant reservations - voice AI goes far beyond basic reception
🔀
8:00 – 14:00

Call Routing, Screening & Handoff

  • Routing in the world of generative AI: how AI-driven routing compares to traditional press-1-press-2 phone trees - dynamic intent detection vs. static menu options, and why generative routing handles edge cases that DTMF systems never could
  • Skills-based routing: "billing → accounting," "appointments → scheduler"
  • Call screening patterns: block spam, prioritize VIPs
  • Human handoff: when to transfer, how to preserve context, the warm-transfer pattern
📊
14:00 – 20:00

Analytics

  • Metrics you should care about: focus on outcomes, not dashboards - track what tells you whether the agent is actually solving problems, not just answering calls
  • Live monitoring: active calls, sentiment, transcription
  • The improvement loop: review recordings → identify failure patterns → update instructions → retest
  • How echowin's Business Compass turns conversations into trends, anomalies, and revenue signals automatically - plus weekly AI-generated digests so you don't have to review every call
💼
20:00 – 25:00

The Agency Business Model

  • How to package and sell voice agents regardless of platform
  • Pricing models: consumption-based (per-minute, per-call) vs. solutions and value-based pricing - charge for outcomes, not just usage. Bundled tiers, annual prepaid, and retainer models
  • Margins: charge 2–5× infrastructure cost; target 60–80% gross margin
  • White-label economics: sub-accounts, client isolation, custom branding
  • The recurring-revenue play: monitoring + maintenance retainers
🛡️
25:00 – 30:00

Limitations, Workarounds & Guardrails

  • What voice agents struggle with today (platform-agnostic)
  • Safety nets: call screening, transfer fallback, SMS follow-up
  • The two-strike rule for tool failures
  • Prompt injection via audio and instruction hierarchy as defense
  • Legal essentials: AI disclosure, call recording consent, HIPAA considerations
🔨
30:00 – 42:00

Build It: Hands-On

  • Step 1 - Instructions: Write instructions using the Greeting → Disambiguation → Resolution → Close pattern (works on every platform)
  • Step 2 - Knowledgebase: Website sync, document upload, FAQ pairs (every platform has an equivalent)
  • Step 3 - Connect tools: Scheduling, SMS, email, webhooks (universal integration patterns)
  • Step 4 - Deploy: Assign a phone number to your agent
  • Step 5 - Test call: Place a live call. Target: first working call within 15 minutes

Q&A + Next Steps
